Jeep Safaris

Conquer the world's most dramatic mountain roads in a rugged 4x4 across Gilgit-Baltistan and beyond.

What Are Jeep Safaris?

A jeep safari is the ultimate way to experience Northern Pakistan's most remote and breathtaking landscapes. Travel in a sturdy 4x4 vehicle along ancient trade routes, high-altitude passes, and rugged mountain trails that no ordinary vehicle can reach. From the vast wildlife plateau of Deosai to the legendary Khunjerab Pass on the China border, each safari route offers a unique blend of adventure, culture, and raw natural beauty. Our experienced local drivers know every turn of these legendary roads, ensuring a safe and unforgettable journey through the Karakoram, Himalayas, and Hindu Kush.
